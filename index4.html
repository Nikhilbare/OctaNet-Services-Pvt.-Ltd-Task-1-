However, much of the fortification was destroyed by a fire in 1828, and today only the foundations and some of the walls remain. Despite its ruined state, Shaniwar Wada remains a popular tourist attraction in Pune, and it is known for its impressive architecture and historical significance. One of the most famous legends is the story of the young Peshwa prince Narayanrao, who was murdered within the walls of the fortification in 1773.
